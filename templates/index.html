Why Choose Paul Thomas PT services?

After 20+ years in the fitness industry, I feel like I have developed a deep understanding of the key barriers to an active healthy lifestyle as well as some of the major motivators to help people make a change.

My goal is to help people realise their potential, and to do so I think it's important that people understand that we all start from different places.
Unfortunately, the rhythm, distraction, obligations, and stress of modern life present lots of obstacles for people to realise their their potential- that's where I come in!

What is The SweatBox?

The SweatBox is what we affectionately call our training facility in the heart of Madrid.

It is our main base where I work with clients, for individual and pair personal training sessions as well as all our initial consultations, nutritional planning, and small group classes.
